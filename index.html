Our Story

Korea Montauk

Fashion Cleaners (formerly Meadlowlark Cleaners) is a family owned and operated neighborhood dry cleaners and laundry drop. With over 30 years experience, Lisa and Peter bring their expert knowledge to give the utmost care for your garments.

Lisa and Peter started their first dry-cleaners in Sayville, New York almost 35 years ago. Since then, they have helped multiple other dry-cleaners get started, and most recently purchased Fashion Cleaners to provide excellent clothing care in the local community.

We inspect and clean your clothes, all on-site, so that you no longer worry. Stop by while you're out running errands or walking your dog. We are new to the neighborhood, so come check us out!

Love --- Lisa & Peter
